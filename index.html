Why I got into Programming

Programming was a happy accident for me, I had played
around with the idea of it for years though I never committed
to it. I then found a community of people who were like me
and considering programming as a career. Through this
community, I found that I enjoyed what coding entailed, but
beyond that, I felt as though I had a support system to help
when I needed it to. Through this group I was introduced to
multiple Coding Bootcamps, Thinkful being one of those, and
decided that I wanted to make a career of something the I
enjoyed but also challenged me to alway learn new concepts
and ideas.
